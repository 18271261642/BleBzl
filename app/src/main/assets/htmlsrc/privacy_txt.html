Phone permissions：

Contacts permissions：

Control phone vibration permissions：
Used to find the function of the mobile phone. After the APP is connected to the bracelet watch, operate the function of finding the mobile phone of the bracelet or watch, and the mobile phone will ring when the phone is found.；
